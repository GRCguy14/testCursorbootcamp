The Clock

Time doesn't stop; it only bends.

We measure what the hour sends—

A tick, a tock, a breath, a beat,

Then morning turns to noon, to night.

The hands go round. We chase or wait.

We build a life, we choose a date.

But in the space between the chimes,

There is a place outside of time.
