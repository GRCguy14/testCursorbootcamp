Agentic AI

You describe the dream; the agent thinks.

It searches code, suggests, and links,

Builds the feature, runs the test—

A pair of minds, one human, one guest.

No more alone against the stack:

You steer the ship, it charts the track.

The joy's the lift, the shared intent—

To ship what's good, together, sent.
